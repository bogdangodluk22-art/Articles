01 · Problem

Recruiting is the most expensive
part of a business that nobody counts

Imagine: 200 resumes come in for a single opening. An HR manager spends 3–4 minutes on each one. That's 12 hours just for the first filter — and the calls haven't even started yet. Then another 40–60 phone screenings. And in the end — 5 people who are actually worth meeting.

80%
of time — on rejections
Most HR effort goes to candidates who definitely won't be a fit. The first screening is filtering, not recruiting.
12 hrs
per selection round*
Estimated: 200 resumes × 3.5 min + 60 short calls × 5 min. Not counting alignment with the team.
3 weeks
average time-to-hire*
While the recruiter sorts through the inbox, a strong candidate has already accepted an offer at another company.
"I'll hire AI — it doesn't get tired, doesn't forget details, and won't miss a good candidate because of a bad mood."
— The MeetHR concept

This isn't one specific HR person's problem. It's a systemic problem. Screening is monotonous, repetitive work. Exactly what AI was built for. MeetHR takes this burden off entirely and returns only those who passed the selection to the manager.

03 · How It Works

From job opening to offer.
Without a single cold call.

01
The candidate finds the opening
ARCA Robotics — public site
The candidate visits the ARCA Robotics site, reads about the company, sees open positions — Robotics Engineer, Computer Vision, Marketing Lead. A click on "Apply" — and the process begins.
Vanilla JSHostinger VPS
02
Personal invitation
Email with a unique token in the link
n8n launches a workflow: GPT-4o generates a personalized letter with the candidate's name and the job title. The link contains a unique token. No template. Every letter is alive.
n8nGPT-4oEmail
03
Personal candidate portal
The site knows who you are and what you want
Using the token in the URL, the system pulls the name, the position, and all the data from the application. The candidate sees their role, the position details, the company culture, and the interview prep. This isn't a form — it's a personal invitation.
SupabaseSHA-256 Auth
04
Choosing an avatar — like in an RPG
Bohdan, Oleksii, Viktoriia, or Julia
Four AI interviewers with different styles and personalities. The candidate chooses who they want to talk to and presses Start. There's no right choice. But there is one you'll feel more comfortable with.
ElevenLabsLoop Video
05
A live AI interview
15–20 minutes. Voice. A real conversation.
ElevenLabs Conversational AI — not a script with answer options, but a genuine conversation. The avatar listens, understands, follows up. The candidate speaks naturally. The system records and analyzes every word.
ElevenLabs AIVoiceReal-time

Automatic analysis and handoff
Oleksii Khvylia will reach out to you within 24 hours
After the call, the QA Monitor analyzes the conversation across 4 criteria and sends a report to the curator in Telegram. Oleksii — a real account with a real profile — personally writes to everyone who passed. Only the selected candidates reach the manager.

Two sites.
Different goals. One shared system.

Most platforms build a single site where you both get acquainted and go through selection. MeetHR separates these tasks. The first site makes you fall in love — the second one selects.

01
Open · Public
ARCA
Robotics
People find it themselves through search and social media. The goal — to make them fall in love with the company and nudge them to apply.
  • The Osiris robot in 3D as the Hero element
  • Mission, values, team culture
  • 4 robots: Osiris, Kira, Atlas-7, Reef
  • 6 open positions with details
  • A minimal application form
  • UA / EN toggle
02
By invitation · Personal
Interview
Portal

09 · Tech Stack

Eight tools.
One well-orchestrated system.

🎙
ElevenLabs
Conversational AI for live interviews. The voices of all 4 avatars, the post-call webhook, real-time transcript.
⚡
n8n (self-hosted)
Orchestration of all workflows. Email with a token, QA analysis, Telegram report, webhook handling. Hostinger VPS.
🤖
GPT-4o
Email personalization, QA analysis of interviews across 4 criteria, offer generation. Via the n8n HTTP node.
🗄
Supabase
Storage of candidates, interview results, statuses. SHA-256 authorization. Employer dashboard.
💬
Telegram
The delivery channel for QA reports from the character Oleksii Khvylia. A real account with a built-up profile.
🌐
Vanilla JS
Two frontends with no frameworks. Hosted on a Hostinger VPS under the domain builtwithai.pro.
🔐
SHA-256 Auth
Candidate and employer authorization. A unique token in the URL for portal personalization.
📱
PWA
The candidate's mobile dashboard installs as an app right from the browser. No App Store.
